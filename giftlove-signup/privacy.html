1. Who We Are

GifLove is a digital greeting card platform based in Ottawa, Ontario, Canada, that allows users to create and share animated greeting cards via shareable links. In this Privacy Policy, "GifLove", "we", "us", and "our" refer to the GifLove service and its operator.

If you have any questions about this policy or how your data is handled, you can contact us at hello@giflove.com.

2. What Data We Collect

Data When collected Why
Name & email address (sender) When you create an account or sign up for early access To identify you and communicate about your cards
Card content (headline, message, signature) When you create a card To render and deliver your card to recipients
Recipient email addresses When you add recipients to a card To deliver the card link by email (if selected)
Purchase information When you complete a payment To fulfil your order and provide receipts. Handled by Paddle — we receive only an order confirmation, not your payment details.
Card open data (IP address, device type, timestamp) When a card link is opened To show senders when their card has been viewed
Usage data (pages visited, browser type) Automatically when you use the site To improve the service and fix issues

We do not collect sensitive personal data (such as health data, financial account details, or government ID numbers) at any point.

3. How We Use Your Data

We use the data we collect for the following purposes only:

  • Delivering your cards — storing card content and generating shareable links
  • Sending email delivery — forwarding card links to recipients where email delivery is selected
  • Open tracking — logging when a card link is opened and reporting this to the sender
  • Order fulfilment — confirming payments and providing purchase records
  • Service improvement — understanding how the site is used in aggregate to fix bugs and improve features
  • Legal compliance — retaining records as required by applicable law

We do not use your data for targeted advertising. We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal data to any third party for marketing purposes.

4. Third-Party Services

GifLove uses a small number of trusted third-party services to operate. Each of these processes data only as necessary to provide their function:

Paddle (Payment Processing)

All payments are processed by Paddle.com, which acts as the Merchant of Record. Paddle collects your name, email address, billing address, and payment method details directly. GifLove does not receive or store this payment information. Paddle is GDPR-compliant and processes data in accordance with its own Privacy Policy.

Email Delivery (SendGrid)

Where you choose to send a card via email, we use SendGrid (a Twilio company) to deliver the card link to your recipients' inboxes. SendGrid processes recipient email addresses solely for this delivery purpose. SendGrid's Privacy Policy applies.

Hosting (SiteGround / Vercel)

Our website and application are hosted on cloud infrastructure providers. These providers may process server logs that include IP addresses as part of standard hosting operations.

We do not use Facebook Pixel, Google Ads, or any third-party advertising trackers on GifLove.

5. Data About Card Recipients

When someone opens a GifLove card link, we collect limited technical data including:

  • The timestamp of when the link was opened
  • Device type (mobile, tablet, or desktop)
  • IP address (used to estimate general location; not stored beyond 30 days)

This data is shared with the card sender so they can see that their card was received and opened. It is not used for any other purpose and is not shared with any third parties.

If you are a recipient and wish to have your open data removed, please contact us at hello@giflove.com.

6. Data Retention

  • Card content (messages, text) — deleted automatically when the card expires, 7 days after creation
  • Recipient email addresses — deleted within 30 days of card expiry
  • Open tracking logs — IP addresses purged after 30 days; aggregate open counts retained for up to 90 days
  • Account and purchase records — retained for as long as your account is active and for up to 7 years thereafter as required for tax and legal compliance
  • Early access sign-ups — retained until you unsubscribe or request deletion

You may request deletion of your personal data at any time by emailing hello@giflove.com. We will action requests within 30 days.

7. Security

We take data security seriously. GifLove uses HTTPS encryption on all pages, stores data in secured databases with access controls, and limits data access to only those who need it to operate the service.

Card links are generated using unique 12-character tokens that are not guessable by brute force. However, as with any shareable link, you should be mindful about who you share your card link with, as anyone in possession of the link can view the card until it expires.

No method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure. While we do our best to protect your data, we cannot guarantee absolute security.

8. Cookies

GifLove uses a minimal number of cookies:

Cookie Purpose Duration
Session cookie Keeps you logged in during your visit Session (deleted when browser closes)
Preference cookie Remembers basic display preferences 30 days

We do not use advertising cookies or third-party tracking cookies. Paddle may set cookies on the checkout overlay for fraud prevention and payment processing purposes — these are governed by Paddle's own cookie policy.

You can disable cookies in your browser settings, though this may affect certain features of the service.

9. Your Rights

Depending on where you are located, you may have the following rights regarding your personal data:

  • Right to access — request a copy of the personal data we hold about you
  • Right to rectification — ask us to correct inaccurate or incomplete data
  • Right to erasure — request that we delete your personal data ("right to be forgotten")
  • Right to restrict processing — ask us to limit how we use your data
  • Right to data portability — receive your data in a structured, machine-readable format
  • Right to object — object to our processing of your data in certain circumstances

To exercise any of these rights, please email hello@giflove.com. We will respond within 30 days. We may ask you to verify your identity before processing your request.

Canadian Residents (PIPEDA)

GifLove is based in Ottawa, Ontario, Canada and is subject to Canada's Personal Information Protection and Electronic Documents Act (PIPEDA) and Ontario's applicable privacy legislation. Under PIPEDA you have the right to access the personal information we hold about you, the right to challenge its accuracy, and the right to withdraw consent for its use (subject to legal and contractual obligations). GifLove does not sell personal information. To exercise your rights under PIPEDA, contact our privacy officer at hello@giflove.com. If you believe your privacy rights have been violated, you may also file a complaint with the Office of the Privacy Commissioner of Canada.

EU / UK Residents (GDPR)

If you are located in the European Economic Area or the United Kingdom, your data is processed in accordance with the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR). Our lawful basis for processing your data is:

  • Contract performance — processing necessary to deliver a card you have purchased
  • Legitimate interests — improving the service, preventing fraud, and maintaining security
  • Legal obligation — retaining financial records as required by law
  • Consent — for marketing communications (early access sign-ups), which you may withdraw at any time

10. Children's Privacy

GifLove is not directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal data from children under 13. If you believe a child under 13 has provided us with personal data, please contact us at hello@giflove.com and we will delete it promptly.

Continued use of GifLove after changes are posted constitutes your acceptance of the revised policy.
